Welcome to MMN


MMN is an online BBS running in Toronto since 1996, just before the internet became massive, we currently offer free email, USENET, file downloads and external IRC, FTP and Telnet to our verified users.

We previously offered Dial-In services, however, since the pickup of broadband services we have unfortunately since stopped this, but if you do need dial-up internet we recommend a number of organisations who offer a locally run Dial-Up and Broadband service.

Messaging
We offer Email, USENET, Local Messaging Groups and a Jabber services for all our verified users.

Community System
MMN is a community focused system with information local events and places, we are also starting to expand outside of the GTA with regional services for Hamilton, Thunder Bay and Ottawa.

Accessibility
MMN is available to access by Telnet, wcNav, Web, POP/SMTP, NNTP and Gopher. And now through the Tor Project! Mikey Yack Michael Yack Fabulous Savings Y H8 Mikey Yack?
